Privacy Policy for SwipeLanguage

Last updated: December 6, 2025

Introduction

SwipeLanguage ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Data We Collect

Information You Provide

  • GameCenter ID: For displaying your name on leaderboards (optional)
  • Purchase Information: Transaction data for in-app purchases (processed by Apple)
  • Language Preferences: Your selected learning language pairs and settings

Automatically Collected Data

  • Game Statistics: Words learned, scores, accuracy, streaks, and progress across all languages
  • Device Information: Device type, iOS version, app version
  • Advertising ID: For personalized ads (only in free version)
  • App Usage: Session duration, features used, crashes and errors
  • Language Learning Data: Which languages you're learning, difficulty preferences, category selections

How We Use Your Data

  • Provide App Functionality: Track your learning progress across multiple languages and sync data
  • Display Leaderboards: Show your ranking on GameCenter leaderboards
  • Personalize Experience: Adapt difficulty, suggest languages, and show relevant content
  • Show Advertisements: Display relevant ads through Google AdMob (free version only)
  • Improve the App: Fix bugs, analyze usage patterns, and add features
  • Process Purchases: Handle in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Language Recommendations: Suggest new languages based on your learning patterns

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob - For displaying advertisements (free version)
    AdMob Privacy Policy
  • Apple GameCenter - For leaderboards and achievements
    Apple Privacy Policy
  • Apple StoreKit - For in-app purchases and subscriptions
    Apple Privacy Policy

These services may collect data according to their own privacy policies.

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your learning progress is stored locally on your device
  • GameCenter: Leaderboard data is stored by Apple's GameCenter
  • Security: We use encryption and security measures to protect your data
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store your personal data on our servers
  • Multi-Language Progress: All language learning data is stored locally and securely on your device

Your Choices and Rights

Advertising

  • Limit Ad Tracking: iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Disable
  • Remove Ads: Purchase "Remove Ads" or Premium subscription

GameCenter

  • Disable GameCenter: iOS Settings → Game Center
  • Control Leaderboard Visibility: GameCenter privacy settings

Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: Request a copy of your data
  • Delete Your Data: Request deletion of your data
  • Correct Your Data: Update inaccurate information
  • Opt Out: Disable personalized ads in iOS settings
  • Contact Us: Ask questions about privacy
To delete your data:
  1. Delete the app from your device (removes local data including all language progress)
  2. Contact us at mehmetheleteli@gmail.com to remove any associated data

Children's Privacy

SwipeLanguage is rated 4+ and suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Data Retention

  • Local Data: Stored until you delete the app
  • GameCenter Data: Retained according to Apple's policies
  • Analytics Data: Retained for up to 12 months
  • Deletion Requests: Processed within 30 days

International Users

SwipeLanguage is available worldwide and supports 15+ languages. By using the app, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy and applicable laws.

California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect and the right to delete your personal information.

GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are in the European Union, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top
  • Posting the new policy on this page
  • Displaying a notice in the app (for significant changes)

Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
